Our technology: The AI driven digital twin

Our hardware-free solution enables you to generate the required data in three steps.

3D Modelling with static data

We model your building and its surroundings in three dimensions. We get started with any data you provide us with and add missing information layer by layer.

Capturing dynamic data with virtual sensors

Our data solutions maximises the visual and thermal comfort of your room conditions. This has several benefits in regards to health and leads to productivity increments of 10%-25%.

Providing data for optimised control

We merge the dynamic and static data threads with our machine learning algorithms to form a digital twin of the building. In the next step, taking advantage of machine learning algorithms the energy consumption of the building is predicted.
